Welcome to my favorite hobby: Table Tennis

We will discuss the basics of the sport, rules of the game, and the history of the sport.

Basics of the sport:

Table Tennis is a fast paced racket sport that players use a small padel to hit a lightweight ball over a net. The goal of the game is to serve the ball between you and your opponent while trying to make the ball unreciporcal for your opponent in order to win a point.

Rules of the game:

Tableable tennis (ping pong) games are played to 11 points, requiring a 2-point lead to win, with matches typically best 3-of-5 or 4-of-7 games. Players alternate serves every 2 points, with the ball must be tossed at least 6 inches, struck behind the table, and bounce on both sides.

History of the Sport:

Table tennis originated in Victorian England during the 1880s as an indoor, upper-class parlour game inspired by lawn tennis, often using cigar box lids for paddles, corks for balls, and books for a net. Originally called "whiff-whaff" or "ping-pong," it gained traction with celluloid balls in 1900, the formation of the ITTF in 1926, and Olympic debut in 1988.
